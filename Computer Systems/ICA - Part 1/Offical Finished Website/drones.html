Amazon is one of the first companies to start testing drones for commercial use.
In the U.S.A,in order for it to start testing it had to comply with the CAA and was able to explore three areas,
1:operations beyond line of sight
2:obstacle avoidance
3:flights where one person operates multiple automonous drones

This has allowed amazon to fully research these areas in order to see how much research is necesarry and if it is feasble. One area which is stil a big hurdle is that uk legislation requires drones to have 500m line of sight,whether they will be able to overcome this is another area but in time this maybe reduced if the benefits outweight the negatives. Pilots as well need training and then apply to the CAA so that they can fly drones for commerical uses.
